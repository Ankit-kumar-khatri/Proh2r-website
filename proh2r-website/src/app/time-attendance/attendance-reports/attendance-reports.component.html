Muster Roll

Attendance Register in Payroll

A Muster Roll is an employment attendance register, about a specific worksite; contractor or employer, and a particular period. It is also used as an acknowledgement, to claim funds from the concerned for the payment of wages.

Monthly Attendance Report

Monthly Attendance Details

The report represents the attendance data of an employee for each day for a whole month. The holidays and weekend will be marked in the report. The report also represents whether the employee was on leave for a particular leave along with the leave type.
